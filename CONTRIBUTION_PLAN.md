# Contribution Plan

## Strategy
My strategy for maximizing accepted PRs involves starting with low-friction tasks. This will allow me to familiarize myself with my peers' CI/CD workflows, coding standards, and communication styles without getting bogged down in complex architecture. 

Before starting any work, I will comment on the issue (e.g., *"Hi! I'd love to work on this issue. I'll submit a PR shortly."*) to prevent overlapping work.
